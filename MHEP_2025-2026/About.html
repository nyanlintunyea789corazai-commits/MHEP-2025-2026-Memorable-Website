A Year Worth Remembering

It still feels unreal to say goodbye after our 10-month journey together.
Time passed so quickly, and yet the memories we created feel deep and unforgettable.

Even now, I can still remember the fresh air of the first day.
Everyone was trying to look cool and calm.
The girls came to school with proper makeup, and the boys wore neat and clean shirts.

We were all a little shy, trying to show our best selves.
But after several days, those makeup looks and nice outfits slowly began to disappear.
The water shortage at our school allowed us to see each other's bare faces, and without even realizing it, we started becoming closer.
That was the moment when strangers slowly turned into friends, and friends turned into family.

We lived the same routine every day in the same compound.
We shared shelter, food, classrooms, dorms, and time.
We studied together, laughed together, worried about exams together, and sometimes even got annoyed with each other.
But all those little things made this place feel like home.
When I look back, I don't only see classmates — I see personalities that made every single day colorful and alive
